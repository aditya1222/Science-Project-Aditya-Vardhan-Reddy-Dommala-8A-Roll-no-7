How PPE Kits are made/What are PPE Kits?:

  • The full form of PPE is Personal Protective Equipment.
  • It is a full body suit used for protection against infectious viruses and harmful foreign substances like radiation.
  • It is used by people working in various fields of expertise that involves hazardous work.
  • In this Covid-19 epidemic, they are the need of the hour especially for hard working doctors that treat patients infected with the virus.
  • It consists of full-length coats, Hand gloves shoes, eye protectors, special masks and respirators to treat patients with minimal contact.
